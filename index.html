About Me

My Young Years

Although I was not at the level of Sheldon from 'The Big Bang Theory', I showed an intrest in programming, science, and engineering since I was very young. When I was little I would collect old keys and test them on difrent locks around the house every day. I knew they would not work but I liked seeing what keys would fit into what keyholes. As I grew up my family got me tools for my birthdays and christmas. I got old hand drills, screwdivers, drill bits, little kits with difrent size bolts and screws. I loved my tools and I started taking old things apart to see how they worked. I never understood anything i saw inside but it still fasinated me. I filled boxes with parts from things I disassembled.

I Get Older

When I was older I got more into programming. I discovered arduinos and raspberry pi's and I did my research and determined that I needed a rpi. Looking back I was young and did not make the right disision. Older me would have gotten younger me a arduino to start out with. I remember the table I sat at as I set up the RPI. I was so happy. When i got it working the first thing I disovered is that it can't be powered back on after shutdown. You have to unplug it and plug it back in. So, I fixed that. I used a button to short gnd and 5v so it would boot back up (not a great idea :/ but I was young).
